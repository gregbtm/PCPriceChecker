Budget Builder

Enter your total budget and use-case. Get a recommended component allocation with suggested parts.

Upgrade Advisor

Enter your current CPU and GPU. Get bottleneck analysis and the best upgrade options within your budget.

Build vs Buy

Compare the cost of self-building to buying a pre-built. Prices are pulled from your tracked components and pre-built watchlist.

Benchmark Compare

Head-to-head PassMark score comparison. Works for any CPU or GPU from the benchmark database — name matching is fuzzy (e.g. "RTX 4070" or "Ryzen 5 7600X").

vs

Deal Scores

Tracked components ranked by how close current price is to all-time low. Requires ≥3 price snapshots.

Best Value per £

Ranks CPUs or GPUs by PassMark score ÷ estimated price within your budget. Uses tier-based price estimates — not live prices.

Compatibility Checker

Enter any component names to check for socket mismatches, memory standard conflicts, PSU wattage, and form-factor issues. Leave blank what you don't have yet.
